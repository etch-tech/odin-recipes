Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Description

Pulled Pork wiches with fries? Yum!

This recipe practically cooks it's self, all we have to do is throw some stuff in a pot and toss some fries in the oven.

Let's get into it!

Ingredients

  • 2 pound bag of frozen fries
  • 2 pounds of pork tenderloin
  • 12 fluid oz of root beer
  • 18 oz barbecue sauce
  • 8 hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour root beer over top.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-7 hours, after that time the pork shreds easily.
  4. Follow instructions on frozen bag of fries, then put the fries in the oven when it will finish simultaneously with the pork.
  5. When the pork is finished cooking and has been shredded, stir and mix barbecue sauce in.
  6. Evenly distribute pork on buns with a side of fries, serve and enjoy!
