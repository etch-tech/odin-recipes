Garlic Fried Chicken

Description

This recipe is fairly simple, and not requiring many steps or ingredients.

In about 30 minutes you can have yourself some tasty fried chicken that pairs nice with almost anything.

After following this recipe try to throw it with some pasta, salad, rice or anything you can think of!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin

Steps

  1. In a shallow dish, mix together the garlic powder, pepper, salt, paprika, bread crumbs, and flour. In another dish, whisk the mik and egg together.
  2. Heat the oil in an electric skillet set to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Dip the chicken into the egg and milk, then evenly coat them with the dry ingredients.
  3. Fry the chicken in the hot oil for about 5 minutes on each side, or until chicken is cook through and juices run clear. Remove with slotted spatula and serve!
